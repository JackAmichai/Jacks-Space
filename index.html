My Story

I've always been drawn to one question: how can we build AI systems that truly understand human context and intent — not just process data?

In the IDF, I served as a Staff Sergeant, leading teams in high-pressure environments. That's where I learned that clear communication, psychological safety, and rapid decision-making are the real infrastructure of any successful mission — and any successful product.

Curiosity about the human mind led me to study Psychology and Computer Science at the Open University of Israel. Between 2021–2023, I worked as a Research Software Engineer at the Technion, managing cross-functional teams and engineering robust backend tools for large-scale cognitive simulations.

Today, I'm delivering Cloud and AI integration strategies at Deloitte, designing SAP BTP integrations and developing Python/FastAPI microservices for enterprise clients. In parallel, I develop proprietary yield management tools for Adi Ohayon Revenue Management and work on independent ventures like Sentinel OS — an ethical AI orchestration layer for elderly care robotics.

The common thread: I specialize in translating complex business needs into scalable technical architectures. Whether it's Multi-Agent Systems, RAG pipelines, or enterprise integrations — I bridge the gap between non-technical stakeholders and engineering constraints.

My vision is to grow into an AI Solutions Architect who bridges technical innovation and real user needs — designing systems that are powerful under the hood, but feel natural, ethical, and obvious to the people using them.
